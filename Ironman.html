My Experience

I got this toy on my 4th birthday which was around the time when the first Iron man movie came out.

I was immediatly attracted to this toy because of its lightup and voice interactions,since i was young back then

Features

-Rigid metal body makes this action figure REALLY hard to break

-All new interactive sound system with buttons

-Iron man isn't Iron man without his lightup suit, so MARVEL added glow in the dark lights which emit from his face and body whenever its dark

-Water-resistant for 30 minutes in 13 meters of water, so if you feel like taking Iron man for a little dive underwater, you can safely do so
